AgentGo — Insurance CRM

Sales Tech Flutter Supabase CRM Integration

CORE FOCUS

Enterprise-grade CRM for Insurance Agents.

MISSION

To streamline client management and networking for high-performance sales teams.

DELIVERY

Client Tracking → Real-time Notifications → Commission Dashboards.

🚀 Overview

AgentGo is a specialized CRM platform built to handle the complex workflows of insurance agents. It provides a unified portal for tracking client lifecycles, managing policy renewals, and coordinating with other agents in a network.

⚙️ Key Features

  • Advanced Client Lifecycle: Deep tracking of leads, active policies, and renewals.
  • Secure Data Handling: Built on Supabase for enterprise-grade security and reliability.
  • Networking Tools: Integrated features for agents to collaborate on complex claims or projects.
  • Real-time Notifications: Immediate alerts for task deadlines and client updates.

💰 Commission Tracking

Custom-built logic to calculate and visualize commissions, giving agents a transparent view of their performance and earnings in real-time.

🌍 Scalability

Designed with a focus on high-performance mobile interactions and a backend capable of supporting thousands of concurrent users across distributed territories.
